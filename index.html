Psychotherapy

I offer a compassionate, direct approach to individual therapy. My role is to support you in reaching toward your best self, the one you may have lost touch with. I want to hear about the problems you see, as well as what is going well in your life. I am an active-listener, supportive and non-judgmental. I will provide an alternate perspective and we will discover the patterns getting in your way.

Individual therapy is highly personalized and tailored to meet your specific needs. I provide short term services for immediate concerns and longer term therapy for chronic issues. You can expect individual therapy sessions to be about 50 minutes. Typically, we will meet once a week or every other week. This will depend on your scheduling needs and what brings you in. During our first session, I will ask a variety of questions intended to focus not only on the primary concerns that bring you in, but also about how your past impacts who you are today. Please remember that our sessions are confidential and any information we exchange is considered protected health information.
